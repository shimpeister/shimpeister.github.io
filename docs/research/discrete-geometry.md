# Discrete Differential Geometry

This theme studies discrete analogues of classical surface theories and their integrable structures.

## Topics

- Discrete constant negative Gaussian curvature surfaces.
- Discrete constant mean curvature surfaces on graphs.
- Discrete affine spheres.
- Nonlinear d'Alembert formulae.
- Dressing actions and integrable discretizations.

## Representative publications

- A construction method for discrete constant negative Gaussian curvature surfaces.
- Nonlinear d'Alembert formula for discrete pseudospherical surfaces.
- Representation formula for discrete indefinite affine spheres.
- Discrete constant mean curvature surfaces on general graphs.
