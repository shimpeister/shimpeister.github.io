# Minimal Lagrangian and Product Geometries

This theme concerns minimal Lagrangian surfaces and related surface theories in complex space forms, para-complex spaces, quadrics, and product geometries.

## Topics

- Minimal Lagrangian surfaces in complex projective and hyperbolic spaces.
- Ruh--Vilms type theorems.
- Loop group methods for quadrics and para-complex projective spaces.
- Willmore surfaces in product spaces.

## Representative publications

- Ruh-Vilms theorems for minimal surfaces without complex points and minimal Lagrangian surfaces in CP^2.
- Timelike minimal Lagrangian surfaces in the indefinite complex hyperbolic two-space.
- Minimal Lagrangian surfaces in the two dimensional complex quadric via the loop group method.
