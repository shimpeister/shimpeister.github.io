# Teichmüller Theory and Harmonic Maps

This theme studies geometric structures related to harmonic maps, Teichmüller theory, and the complex landslide flow. It develops integrable-systems methods as a tool for studying geometric deformation problems.

## Topics

- Complex landslide flow.
- Harmonic maps into hyperbolic two-space.
- Constant Gaussian curvature surfaces in hyperbolic three-space.
- Connections with Teichmüller theory and geometric transitions.

## Representative publications

- The complex landslide flow and the method of integrable systems.
- A classification of constant Gaussian curvature surfaces in the three-dimensional hyperbolic space.
