# Statistical, Hessian, and Affine Geometry

This theme studies affine and statistical geometric structures, including alpha-connections, Hessian geometry, and geometric aspects of optimization.

## Topics

- Statistical manifolds of normal and multivariate normal distributions.
- Alpha-connections and constant curvature statistical manifolds.
- Hessian geometry and convex optimization.
- Affine differential geometry and symmetric cones.
- Projectively flat affine connections and parallel cubic forms.

## Representative publications

- A characterization of the alpha-connections on the statistical manifold of normal distributions.
- On a constant curvature statistical manifold.
- Geodesics of multivariate normal distributions and a Toda lattice type Lax pair.
- A characterization of the alpha-connections on the statistical manifold of multivariate normal distributions.
